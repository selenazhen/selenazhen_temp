checkout flow

The checkout flow was designed for the user's simplicity, by changing checkout into a "divide and conquer" style between the gift giver and the recipient. The only thing that the gift giver needs to provide is their billing information and the recipient's email, and the recipient will provide their own mailing address.

STEP 1

Once they have chosen a product, the gift giver enters the recipient's information and gift message.

STEP 2

Gift giver enters their payment information.

STEP 3

Gift giver receieves a confirmation email, letting them know that their gift request has been sent to the recipient.

STEP 4

The recipient receives an email notifying them of their gift and is given a link to enter their own shipping information to receive their gift.

STEP 5

Recipient enters their shipping information.

STEP 6

Recipient congratulations page; their gift is on its way!
